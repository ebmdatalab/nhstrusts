Conflicts of Interest Disclosures at NHS Trusts

Assessing transparency of conflict of interest declarations in English NHS trusts

About this site

We wanted to know how transparent NHS trusts were about the conflicts of interest of their employees, so we requested Gifts and Hospitality Registers from all Trusts in England for 2015-16, under the Freedom of Information Act. This table shows what - if anything - we got back from each Trust.
